What is MathMonke?

MathMonke is an app designed to help users boost their math skills efficiently and enjoyably. With a user-friendly interface, it allows learners to choose their starting level and rewards progress with XP for every lesson completed.


Customized Learning: Start at the level that matches your current math skills.

🙉

🙊

Progress Tracking: Earn XP for every lesson completed to track your improvement.

Engaging Experience: The gamified approach makes learning math fun and motivating.
